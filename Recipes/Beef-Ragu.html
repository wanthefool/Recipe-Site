Beef Ragu Recipe

    Ingredients
  • Chuck roast
  • Celery
  • Carrots
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Bay Leaf
  • Tomato Paste
  • Worshestershire Sauce
  • Salt and Pepper
  • pasta
  • cream
    Steps
  • Sautee onion, carrot and celery
  • Add and cook off tomato paste
  • Add bay leaf
  • Cube beef and brown in pan
  • Add water
  • Add Worshestershire sauce and braise on low heat
  • add cream and put on low heat
  • add salt and pepper to taste
  • Shred beef with forks
  • boil pasta with salted water
  • add pasta water to sauce and stir until emulsified
  • serve sauce and pasta
