Roast Duck Recipe

    Ingredients
  • Duck
  • salt
  • Pepper
  • scallions
  • ginger
  • salt
  • honey
  • soy sauce
  • five spice
    Steps
  • boil scallions, ginger, salt and pepper until fragrant
  • boil duck in the solution until internal temp reacher 135 degrees fahrenheit
  • pat dry and hang duck to dry for 4 hours
  • make a marinade of soy sauce, honey, salt and five spice powder
  • brush the duck all over with the marinade, making sure that all crevices are covered
  • let marinade for an hour
  • stuff duck cavity with more ginger and scaallions
  • place duck on rack and roast at 375 degrees fahrenheit for 90 minutes
  • baste with marinade every 20 minutes
  • once done, allow duck to rest for half hour before serving
