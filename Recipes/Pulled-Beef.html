Pulled Beef Recipe

    Ingredients
  • Chuck roast
  • Celery
  • Carrots
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Bay Leaf
  • Tomato Paste
  • Worshestershire Sauce
  • Salt and Pepper
    Steps
  • Sautee onion, carrot and celery
  • Add and cook off tomato paste
  • Add bay leaf
  • Cube beef and brown in pan
  • Add water
  • Add Worshestershire sauce and braise on low heat
  • add salt and pepper to taste
  • once beef is pull apart tender, turn off the heat and rest for half an hour
  • Shred beef with forks
